About Aeye



  • Most of the current video surveillance systems rely upon human operators for proactive response. Research has shown that human concentration drops by 90% after 22 minutes of viewing. Thus, 95% of all video goes unmonitored.

  • Hardware based solutions can be expensive.

  • Aeye is the solution to this problem.Using artificial intelligence it makes video surveillance intelligent and cost-effective. It provides features like Intrusion Detection,Permiter Monitoring,Camera Tampering Detection etc that make surveillance easy and efficient.
